The Legacy

Established 1994

A heritage of subversion and grace.

Founded in the heart of Milan by visionary artisan Elena Rostova, AURA began as a small, secretive atelier catering exclusively to the avant-garde elite. We rejected the mass production of the 90s, focusing instead on architectural silhouettes and the revival of ancient weaving techniques.

Every garment is a rebellion against the ordinary. We source our silks from centuries-old mills in Como and our wool from sustainable, high-altitude farms. True luxury is time, patience, and the touch of a master's hand.
